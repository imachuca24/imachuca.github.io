Instructional Skills Workshop (ISW)

  • Date: March, 2018
  • Role: Workshop participant
  • Description: The Instructional Skills Workshop (ISW) is hosted by the Centre for Teaching, Learning and Technology (CTLT) and is recognized by many universities as an intructional development initiative. The workshop is comprised of various theme modules designed to strenthen pedagogical skills through intensive practical exercises in learning-centred teaching.
  • Modules Completed:
    • Learning objectives
    • Active learning techniques
    • Collaborative learning techniques
    • Knowledge assessment techniques
    • Experiential learning
    • Inclusive classrooms
    • Challenging classroom situations
    • Teaching portfolios
  • Related Experiences: EOSC 516 (Teaching and Learning in Earth, Ocean and Atmospheric Science)
