About Me

Home / About Me

Idalia A. Machuca

I am a master's student in the Earth, Ocean, and Atmospheric Sciences department at the University of British Columbia. My area of specialization is Physical Oceanography, and I conduct research with the Canyons group lead by Dr. Susan Allen. Our research explores the complex oceanic flows found in submarine canyons.

Previous to this appointment, I received my bachelor's degree in Geophysics from the University of British Columbia and worked as a research assistant with the Salish Sea Modelling Group.
